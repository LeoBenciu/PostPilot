Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

PostPilot ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: name, email, authentication identifiers.
  • Usage data: interactions with the app, settings, and feature usage.
  • Connected platform data: profile and post performance data you authorize.
  • Billing data: subscription status and Stripe identifiers (not full card details).

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve the PostPilot service.
  • Generate AI coaching responses and analytics insights.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Ensure security, prevent fraud, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis

We process data based on contract performance, legitimate interest, legal obligations, and consent where required.

4. Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers necessary to operate PostPilot (for example, hosting, authentication, analytics, payment processing, and AI providers), and only under appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for service operation, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement of agreements.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, deletion, export, or restriction of your personal data.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data.

8. Contact

For privacy requests, contact us at privacy@postpilotcoach.com.
