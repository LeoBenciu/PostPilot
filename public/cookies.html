Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how PostPilot uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our website and application.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember preferences and improve your experience.

2. Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: authentication, security, and session management.
  • Analytics cookies: understand usage and improve product decisions.
  • Preference cookies: remember settings such as language preferences.

3. Third-Party Technologies

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics, payments, and infrastructure that set their own cookies according to their policies.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may impact functionality (for example authentication and secure sessions).

5. Contact

For questions about this policy, contact privacy@postpilotcoach.com.
